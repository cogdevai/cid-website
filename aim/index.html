How It Works

From ambition to action,
in four steps

Aim uses behavioral science and AI to create a plan that actually fits your life. No generic advice — just a roadmap built for you.

1

Name your goal

Type any goal — learn a language, run a marathon, launch a business. Add context about your situation so Aim can personalize everything.

2

Answer a few questions

Aim's AI asks about your timeline, availability, and preferences. It uses this to craft a plan grounded in behavioral science — not guesswork.

3

Get your personalized plan

Aim generates a structured roadmap of milestones, each broken into concrete subtasks with clear timelines. Your entire journey, mapped out.

4

Track and achieve

Check off subtasks daily, watch your progress build milestone by milestone. Aim keeps you moving forward with clarity and momentum.
