Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

TinyTaste ("the App") is operated by Cognitive Intelligence Development ("CID", "we", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following data that you voluntarily provide:

  • Baby profile information: Name, date of birth, due date (optional), weaning start date, milk feeding type
  • Food and milk logs: Foods logged, reactions, textures, amounts, meal times, notes
  • Supplement tracking: Supplement names and daily check-off status
  • Journal entries: Freeform text entries you write
  • Account data: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalised nutrition insights and recipe suggestions
  • To generate AI-powered developmental insights based on your baby's age
  • To track nutrition patterns and identify gaps in your baby's diet
  • To sync your data across devices (if signed in)
  • To improve the App through anonymised analytics

4. Data Storage

Your food logs, milk logs, journal entries, and supplement data are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you sign in, subscription status is stored in Google Firebase Firestore. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. AI Processing

When you use intelligent features (recipe generation, insights), your baby's food log data is sent to our backend server for processing via Anthropic's Claude. This data is used solely to generate personalised content and is not stored on their servers after processing. Anthropic does not use your data for training.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: Authentication, Firestore (subscription data), Analytics, Crashlytics
  • Anthropic Claude AI: Recipe and insight generation
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple, In-App Purchases, App Store

7. Children's Privacy

TinyTaste is designed for parents and caregivers, not for use by children directly. The baby profile data you enter is under your control as the parent. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings > Delete Account in the App. This permanently removes your data from both the device and our servers.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at tinytaste@cogdev.ai for any data requests.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.
