Questions

Frequently asked.

What does TinyTaste do?

+

TinyTaste tracks your baby's meals, milk feeds, and supplements. It shows a nutrition wheel of what food groups and nutrients baby has had, provides personalised AI recipes, and gives developmental insights based on your baby's age.

Is TinyTaste free?

+

Yes. TinyTaste is free to download with unlimited food and milk logging, nutrition wheel, supplements, and journal. Premium unlocks AI recipes, smart insights, weekly guidance, and food personality cards for $4.99/month.

What age is TinyTaste for?

+

TinyTaste is designed for babies from 0 months to around 6 years — covering the entire weaning journey from first milk feeds to family meals.

Is the nutrition advice evidence-based?

+

Yes. All nutrition guidance is based on peer-reviewed research from the AAP, WHO, NHS, and EFSA, with references from 2020 or later.
